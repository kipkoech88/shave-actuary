ABOUT NGOPISLURR

Ngopislurr was founded by different individuals with same passion of bringing great tasting coffee and delicious food. We believe that everyone deserves a hearty brunch and decent coffee to enjoy each day.

Using only the freshest ingredients and locally roasted coffee beans, we envision bringing together foreign and local flavours without compromising taste and quality. Our semi casual dining concept allows our team to be in contact with our patrons with the intention of bringing the best experience possible there is.
